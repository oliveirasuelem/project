All Nails Studio Services

  • The Bride Experience

    For The Bride Experience you will be welcomed by our team with a glass of proceco and delicious gourmet sweets. There will be a bridal nail inspiration palette. When scheduling, you can choose between Gel Polish or Overlay (BIAB) in both treatments are includes cuticle work, nail shaping followed color of your choice and a design. To finish, an exfoliation and hydration will be done with our collagen gloves.

  • Manicures

    Treat your hands to a luxurious manicure that includes expert nail shaping, cuticle care, and a choice of regular or gel polish application. Our skilled technicians will ensure your nails look perfectly groomed and polished.

  • Pedicures

    Relax and unwind with our rejuvenating pedicure services. Sit back in our comfortable spa chairs as we pamper your feet with a soothing soak, exfoliation, nail shaping, and a foot massage. Complete the experience with a beautiful polish of your choice.

  • Nail Extensions

    Transform the length and shape of your natural nails with our high-quality nail extensions. Choose from acrylic nails, gel nails, or dip powder nails to achieve the desired look, whether it's natural and subtle or bold and glamorous.

  • Nail Art

    Unleash your creativity with our stunning nail art designs. Our talented nail artists can bring your imagination to life, offering a variety of artistic options, including intricate designs, sparkling embellishments, and personalized patterns that reflect your unique style.

  • Nail Treatments

    Maintain the health and strength of your nails with our nourishing nail treatments. From cuticle care and nail strengthening to hydrating therapies, we offer solutions to improve the condition of your nails and keep them looking their best.
